Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Prime8 ("the Bot", "we", "us") is a Discord bot that integrates with Google Workspace services, including Gmail and Google Calendar, to help users manage their productivity from within Discord. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Prime8, we may access the following information through Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication:

  • Your Google email address and basic profile information
  • Gmail messages and metadata (when you use email-related commands)
  • Google Calendar events and schedules (when you use calendar-related commands)
  • Your Discord user ID (to associate your account with your Google session)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the Bot's functionality:

  • To display your emails and calendar events within Discord
  • To create, modify, or delete calendar events on your behalf
  • To send or reply to emails on your behalf when you issue a command

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to the Bot's core features.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Prime8 does not permanently store your Google data. OAuth tokens are stored securely and are used only to maintain your authenticated session. When you disconnect your account or revoke access, all associated tokens are deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is transmitted only between Discord, Google's APIs, and the Bot's server to perform requested actions.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure storage of OAuth tokens. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke Prime8's access to your Google account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page. You may also contact us to request deletion of any stored data.

8. Google API Services User Data Policy

Prime8's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

Prime8 is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
